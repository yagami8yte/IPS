Place your advertisement video here.

Expected file name: WelcomeVideo.mp4
(or update the WelcomeViewModel.cs to use a different filename)

Supported formats:
- MP4 (.mp4)
- AVI (.avi)
- MOV (.mov)
- WMV (.wmv)

The video will:
- Play automatically when the welcome screen loads
- Loop continuously
- Display in the center of the welcome screen with rounded corners
- Scale to fit the video frame while maintaining aspect ratio

Video recommendations:
- Portrait orientation (1080x1920 or similar ratio)
- Duration: 15-30 seconds for optimal customer engagement
- File size: Keep under 50MB for smooth playback
